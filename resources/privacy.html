Privacy Policy

Michael Nisi’s Privacy Policy was updated on May 25, 2018

Your privacy is as important to me as my own. I’m a person who writes Software, I neither have means nor interest to store huge amounts of data. Thus, the software I build is designed to collect as little data as possible. I’m committed to keeping your personal information safe. OK, that last sentence is Apple’s, I don’t store personal information, they do that for me. I rely on Apple’s strict policies.

Personal Information

Building iOS apps, I don’t require personal information or logins, using iCloud I can build personalized apps without knowing a thing about my users. Apple’s iCloud handles authentication for me.

Application Data

Application data, like the content of the queue in a podcast app, for example, is also stored, and synced across devices, using iCloud. Again, no personal information is accessible to me.
