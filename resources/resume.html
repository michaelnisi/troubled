Work History

  • Podest Podcast App

    2015 - 2018 Developer/Founder, Walsrode

    I wrote Podest, a modest podcast client for iOS, focusing on performance and efficiency. Caching data remotely and locally makes searching and browsing a snappy and unintrusive experience. Podest’s minimalist user interface sets the stage for awesome podcasts. Building an app of this complexity alone and shipping it has been challenging. Picking up Swift along the way, a language in the making, sounds harder than it was. The cognitive interplay between JavaScript, on the server, and Swift proved itself as surprisingly fruitful.
