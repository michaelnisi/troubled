Oh hey, thanks for your interest. The Troubled Programmer is written by me, my name is Michael Nisi, I am a programmer. After years of OOP in mainly ActionScript, these days my main interest lies in concurrency and functional programming.

In regards of languages, I'm into JavaScript, C, Objective-C and Erlang.

The two platforms I'm really excited about these days are Node.js and iOS.

I love the web; therefore I am a great believer in web standards and responsive design. Here I write to learn and to share.

If you are interested you could follow me on Twitter, fork me on GitHub or have a look at my photos on Flickr. Also you could watch images and videos I like on Zootool and Vimeo.
